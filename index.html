ARCS Model and its application in e-learning

ARCS (Keller) is a motivational design framework that helps you capture attention, build relevance, support confidence, and reinforce satisfaction—especially in online learning.

Learning Objectives

Required section

By the end of this lesson, you should be able to:

1) Explain the ARCS Model

Describe the four components and why they matter for motivation.

2) Identify key strategies

Recognize sub-categories and practical tactics for A, R, C, and S.

3) Apply ARCS to e-learning

Propose design decisions that improve engagement and persistence online.

4) Evaluate design quality

Use ARCS as a checklist to critique or improve an online lesson.
