Resources & References

This page lists key references used to explain the ARCS Model and support the application examples in this website. Resources are grouped into academic sources, course materials, and media used for learning support.

A) Academic Sources

Core theory
  1. Keller, J. M. (2010). Motivational Design for Learning and Performance: The ARCS Model Approach. Springer.
  2. Keller, J. M. (1987). Development and use of the ARCS Model of instructional design. Journal of Instructional Development. (Foundational model article.)
  3. Keller, J. M. (2008/2010). ARCS-based motivational design strategies for instruction. (Conceptual and applied strategy guidance across A/R/C/S.)

Note: In this website, the sub-categories (e.g., perceptual arousal, goal orientation, personal control) are presented as practical design lenses rather than as abstract terms.

Learning support
