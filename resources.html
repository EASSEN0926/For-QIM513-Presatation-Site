Resources & References

This page lists the academic and instructional resources used to develop the ARCS Model overview, its application in e-learning, and the assessment activities presented on this website.

Core ARCS Model Literature

  • Keller, J. M. (1987). Development and use of the ARCS model of instructional design. Journal of Instructional Development, 10(3), 2–10.

    Foundational article introducing the ARCS Model and its motivational framework.

  • Keller, J. M. (2010). Motivational design for learning and performance: The ARCS model approach. New York, NY: Springer.

    Comprehensive understanding of ARCS components, sub-categories, and design strategies.

ARCS Model in E-learning

  • Keller, J. M., & Suzuki, K. (2004). Learner motivation and e-learning design: A multinationally validated process. Journal of Educational Media, 29(3), 229–239.

    Application of the ARCS Model to online and technology-enhanced learning environments.

  • Hartnett, M. (2016). Motivation in online education. Singapore: Springer.

    Explores motivation challenges in online learning and supports the relevance of ARCS-based design.
